Closing Verse

Walking upon lotus blossoms,
the Buddha has given the world a lamp of the spirit.

Far from clinging to ego and ignorance,
this planet is but a realm of peace.

We remember the Buddha’s grace across a thousand lifetimes;
we venerate the Buddha — a thousand words could never say enough.

Namo the Great Heroic, the Great Powerful, the Great Compassionate, the Great Merciful, Our Original Teacher Shakyamuni Buddha.
